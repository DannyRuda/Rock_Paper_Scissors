Rock Paper Scissors

How to Play

You play as the blue haired human and your enemy is the red robot. Each round, you click on one of the Choices under your character to chose wether you want to use Rock, Paper, or Scissors this round. The robot will make his choice simultaniously with you. For each win, the Progress bar at the top will move one step towards the looser. The game is over, when the bar is entirely blue or red, indicating the color of the winner.
